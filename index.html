Easygoing and friendly, I will fit well into any team. My constant desire to keep learning and pushing myself makes me an ideal programmer and employee.

My Programming Path

My interest in programming grew out of a conversation in an unlikely place: the backcountry of Colorado. After graduating college, I worked at a lovely public library for almost four years while continuing graduate courses in information science. Although I enjoyed the community-oriented nature and big picture goals of the institution, I began to get bored with my daily work.

I decided to switch things up, and live outside for the rest of the year to achieve one of my other life-goals: being an acting conservationist. After spending the summer and fall doing field work, I took a roadtrip through Colorado. Around a campfire one evening, I met a previous Epicodus student and we talked through the evening about his education and work.

This is when I began to realize that programming had the potential to provide me with the challenging, creative, and fullfilling career I'd been searching for. That winter I began teaching myself to code, and I enrolled in Epicodus in the following spring. I love programming, and am excited to apply my skills at a company in the near future.
